## 🔥 How Viral Sharing Works

### Step 1

Person A shares their Card-io profile with Person B using NFC.

### Step 2

Person B saves the card.

### Step 3

Person B shares the card with friends C, D, and E.

### Step 4

Those users continue sharing the card.

### Step 5

Person A tracks the spread through analytics.

**Example Result**

```text
Your card reached:
47 people
12 shares
4 network levels
Viral coefficient: 1.3
```

This creates organic customer acquisition without recurring advertising spend.
